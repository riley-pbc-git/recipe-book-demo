Nana’s Pancakes

1 egg

1 cup buttermilk

2 TB canola oil

1 cup flour

1 TB sugar

1 tsp baking powder

1/2 tsp baking soda

1/2 tsp salt

(Add blueberries, chopped)

Blend wet ingredients.

Mix dry ingredients together.

Add to wet ingredients; just until blended.

Add milk as needed if too thick.
